Elko's Only Authentic Irish Pub

Welcome to Charlee's Bar, Elko's only authentic Irish pub. Whether you're joining us for karaoke every night, enjoying live music, or relaxing with friends over a glass of premium whiskey, Charlee's offers a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

Located in the heart of Elko, Nevada, we're proud to bring a taste of Irish hospitality to our community. With a dance floor, outdoor stage, and a food truck often parked outside, there's always a reason to stick around. From first-time visitors to regulars, Charlee's is a place to gather, celebrate, and create memorable nights.

Come for the music, stay for the craic.

Raise a Glass

At Charlee's, we're proud to offer something truly rare in Elko — a whiskey wall featuring 180+ bottles, including rare and hard-to-find whiskeys. From classic Jameson pours to bottles you won't find anywhere else in Nevada, our collection speaks for itself.

Whether you're a seasoned whiskey enthusiast or just exploring Irish spirits for the first time, our staff will help you find the perfect pour. Great drinks, great company, and genuine Irish hospitality — every night.
