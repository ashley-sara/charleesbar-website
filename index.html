Elko's Only Authentic Irish Pub

Welcome to Charlee's Bar, Elko's only authentic Irish pub. Whether you're joining us for karaoke every night, enjoying live music, or relaxing with friends over a glass of premium Irish whiskey, Charlee's offers a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

Located in the heart of Elko, Nevada, we're proud to bring a taste of Irish hospitality to our community. With a dance floor, outdoor stage, and a food truck often parked outside, there's always a reason to stick around. From first-time visitors to regulars, Charlee's is a place to gather, celebrate, and create memorable nights.

Come for the music, stay for the craic.
